Hey there! I’m glad you stopped by!

My name is Daniele , I’m a 21 years old student from Lithuania , studying 3rd semester of Multimedia Design at KEA - Copenhagen School of Design and Technology in Copenhagen. If you were wondering what am I like , let me give you a small brief - I am very social, cannot spend a day alone in my house! I love to create and come up with different ideas. Very multicultural person. Have lived and travelled in many places. Helping people is something that drives me to become a better version of myself. Best rule to follow - communication is the key.

For as long as I can remember, from day one, I was drawn to creativity and arts. I would usually express myself through different forms of art - painting, drawing, writing and so on. Since years have been changinging not by hours, but by minutes, I knew that in order to contribute towards the world's improvement, I had to find a new form of expression. Yes, you guessed it - I came across multimedia design. Multimedia design at KEA not only was able to provide me with different kinds of knowledge but also helped to open other doors. I was able to gain basic skills for coding to online marketing and working with different clients in groups.
